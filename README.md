# PathWay AI

## Overview

PathWay AI is an AI-powered educational platform designed to make quality learning accessible to students, particularly those in rural and underserved communities where internet connectivity is often limited. The platform combines AI-assisted learning, mentorship, assessments, resume generation, and career guidance into a unified ecosystem.

One of the key highlights of the platform is its integration with **Ollama** and **Llama 3.1**, enabling AI-powered features such as tutoring and resume generation to run locally without relying on continuous cloud connectivity.

---

## Problem Statement

Many students face barriers such as:

- Limited access to qualified teachers and mentors
- Poor internet connectivity
- Lack of personalized educational support
- Limited career guidance and placement preparation

PathWay AI addresses these challenges by providing an AI-powered learning platform that continues to support learners even in low-connectivity environments.

---

## User Roles

The platform supports three user roles:

- Student
- Teacher
- Peer Mentor

Each role has a dedicated dashboard designed for its specific responsibilities.

---

## Student Dashboard

The student dashboard serves as the primary learning workspace.

### Learning Analytics

Students can monitor their assessment performance and learning progress through an analytics dashboard.

### Offline AI Tutor

The AI Tutor is powered by **Ollama** and **Llama 3.1**, enabling students to receive AI-assisted academic guidance even without continuous internet connectivity.

### AI Quiz Generator

Students can generate quizzes based on:

- Selected topic
- Preferred difficulty level

This enables personalized practice across multiple subjects.

### Find a Mentor

Students requiring additional assistance can connect with mentors through the platform for a nominal mentoring fee.

### Credentials

Students earn credentials based on quiz performance and learning achievements.

### Browse Opportunities

Students can explore available opportunities and openings through the platform.

### AI Resume Builder

Students enter their academic and professional details, after which Ollama generates a professionally formatted resume tailored to the provided information.

---

## Teacher Dashboard

### Student Performance Analytics

Teachers can monitor assessment performance and gain insights into student progress through visual analytics.

### Virtual Mentoring

Teachers can conduct online mentoring sessions with students using integrated video calling functionality.
